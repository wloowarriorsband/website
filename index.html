WARRIORS BAND

Loud is good, fast is better, loud and fast is best!

We are the official pep band for University of Waterloo! Our job is to attend sports games and hype up the crowd with our music. Even though the band was created for attending sports games we attend other events on campus and around the city!

REQUIREMENTS

Pay the membership fee of $10 and attend 2 rehersals per term. The membership fee covers both the main band and drumline. No instrument or experience is required!

DRUMLINE

Taking inspiration from organizations such as DCI and WGI the Warriors Band Drumline plays battery percussion pieces at sporting events and other events around campus and the city. The Warriors Band Drumline is open to people of all experience whether you have played percussion for years or have never touched a drum, come join us at one of our two rehearsals every week to play some drums!

REQUIREMENTS

Pay the membership fee of $10 and print off your music. The membership fee covers both the main band and drumline. We provide all drums and equipment, but bringing your own sticks is encouraged.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I become a full member?

Simply pay a 10$ termly membership fee, and attend at least 2 practices! 100% of membership fees go towards our instruments, equipment, and other band benefits

Do I have to come to practice?

No! We recognise that you're busy, so attendance is always optional, though strongly encouraged. You're also welcome to come late or leave early.

Do I have to come to events?

No! People often come to just part of sports games, so you can come for just one or two periods and then go work on the work term report that you haven't started and is due at midnight tonight.

Do I have to audition?

No! We have never held auditions, and likely never will.

Do I have to practice the music at home?

No. In fact, practising at home is discouraged for fear that one day we might take ourselves seriously.

Where else do you perform?

We usually go to sports games on weekends during the varsity season. During fall terms, we typically march in 2-3 Santa Claus parades. We also invent our own events such as the Halloween march around campus!
